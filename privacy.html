Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Anonymous Authentication: We use Firebase Auth in anonymous mode—no email or personal identifiers are collected.
  • Usage Analytics: We log in-game events (e.g. “game_start”, “game_over”) via Firebase Analytics to understand and improve gameplay.
  • Leaderboard Data: When you submit a high score, we store only your chosen display name and score in Firebase Firestore.
  • Local Storage: We store settings (volume level, chosen bird skin), game state (best score, coins, adventures remaining) in your browser’s localStorage.

2. Cookies & Similar Technologies

We do not use any browser cookies. All persistent data is stored via localStorage. We may use session cookies only for Firebase’s internal operation, but they contain no personal information.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase by Google: Authentication, Firestore database, and Analytics. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Google AdSense: We display ads served by Google. AdSense may collect non-identifying usage information (e.g. ad impressions). See Google Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To enable global leaderboards and competition.
  • To track and improve game performance and balance.
  • To remember your preferences and progress between sessions.
  • To serve relevant ads via AdSense so we can fund game hosting and future updates.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We never sell or share your personal data. Leaderboard entries are public scores (name & score). Firebase and Google may process your information under their own privacy policies as described above.

6. Children’s Privacy

This game is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You may disable analytics by blocking Firebase via browser extensions or network settings.
  • Clear Local Data: You can clear your localStorage in your browser settings to reset game data and preferences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. We’ll post the new effective date at the top. Continued use of the game after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact Us
