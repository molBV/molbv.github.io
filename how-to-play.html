How to Play

Tap, time, and thread hazards. Survive waves, beat bosses, and upgrade.

Controls

  • Space/Click/Tap — Flap
  • P — Pause
  • M — Mute

Power-ups

Shield: one hit protection. Magnet: pulls cores. Boost: short invulnerable dash.

Boss Tips

Learn patterns. Save cores for phase transitions. Micro-taps keep altitude without drift.

Scoring

Distance + cores + boss clears. Perfect phases grant multipliers.
