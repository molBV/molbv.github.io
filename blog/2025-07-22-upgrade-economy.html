Upgrades & economy: making progression feel earned

July 22, 2025 • 7–8 min read

Sources vs. sinks

Cores come from distance, pickups, and boss clears. Sinks include permanent upgrades, one-run consumables, and cosmetic unlocks. The loop should always tempt you, never force you.

Guardrails

  • No upgrade should one-shot a boss or bypass a mechanic.
  • Costs scale gently (sub-exponential) to avoid dead ends.
  • We cap stacking effects to protect readability.

Persistence

Unlocks and progress live in localStorage for now; cloud/leaderboards are planned with Firebase as an opt-in.
