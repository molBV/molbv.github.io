Skins, achievements, and a small in-game shop (without ruining the vibe)

August 5, 2025 • 6–7 min read

Cosmetics first

Skins are purely visual and priced so you can unlock one or two per active week without grinding. No gameplay stat boosts.

Achievements

Goals nudge variety: perfect phase, no-hit wave, magnet chain, etc. They award small core bonuses or cosmetics, not power spikes.

Shop rules

  • Prices scale gently; seasonal items rotate but never FOMO lock forever.
  • Everything purchasable is also earnable.
  • Purchases and unlocks persist via localStorage.
