About Ultimate Birdie

Ultimate Birdie (aka Aviar: Rocket Birdie) is an HTML5 Canvas game that takes the classic “flap to dodge pipes” formula and supercharges it with power-ups, story achievements and epic boss battles. Dive into three distinct modes and see how far you can soar!

Game Modes

  • Adventure – 20 lives per cycle, earn & spend coins to buy extra plays, unlock hidden story events.
  • Marathon – Endless pipe runs, infinite scoring, global leaderboards.
  • Gauntlet – Face mecha boss after boss in a true test of skill and timing.

How to Play

  • Click, tap or press Space to flap your wings.
  • Collect coins to unlock power-ups: double coins, magnets, triple rockets, heavy load and more.
  • Smash through pipes when you’re “super” or spend coins to break obstacles.
  • Spin the roulette wheel for random boosts—push your luck for epic advantages.
  • Track your high scores on the global leaderboard powered by Firebase Firestore.

Built With

  • Pure JavaScript + HTML5 Canvas for smooth, cross-platform play.
  • Firebase Auth & Firestore for anonymous sign-in and global leaderboards.
  • Firebase Analytics for in-game event tracking.
  • CSS3 glassmorphism & Google Fonts for a clean, modern UI.
